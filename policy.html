Richfield Policies

Artisan Warranty

Every structural frame is backed by a 12-year limited warranty, ensuring your investment stands the test of time.

Global Logistics

We provide specialized climate-controlled shipping to over 40 countries, protecting delicate fabrics and wood finishes.

Ethics & Origin

All leathers and timbers are ethically sourced from sustainable European forests and certified tanneries.

The Return Architecture

At Richfield, we understand that true comfort is subjective. If a piece does not resonate with your space, our return process is designed for minimal friction.

01

Curation Review

Submit a request within 30 days. Our team will review the item's condition via a brief digital consultation.

02

Professional Retrieval

We do not expect you to pack heavy furniture. Our specialized couriers will arrive to professionally wrap and collect the item.

03

Refund Issuance

Once received at our atelier, your refund is processed within 48 hours to the original payment method.

Cancellations & Bespoke Orders

Items from our "Custom Archive" or "Bespoke Series" are handcrafted specifically for your dimensions. Cancellations for these items are only accepted within the first 24 hours of the order being placed.

After 24 hours, materials are cut and prepared, and the order becomes final sale.

Still Have Questions?

Our dedicated concierge team is available 24/7 to assist with your inquiries.
