01 / Our Belief

A chair isn't just furniture.
It's where life happens.

The world's greatest ideas were thought from a seated position. The deals that shaped industries, the novels that moved generations, the code that changed everything — all born in a chair. We take that seriously.

Richfield was founded on a single, radical belief: that the seat beneath you should work as hard as you do — silently, perfectly, for decades.

02 / Our Story

From Karachi
to the world.

It started in a small studio in Defence Housing Authority, Karachi. Our founder had a problem: every chair he owned failed within two years. So he built one himself — and never looked back.

Word spread across Karachi, then Lahore, then globally. By 2010, Richfield chairs were in corporate offices across 40 countries.

Today we curate the finest ergonomic chairs on earth — because great seating shouldn't require a custom order.
